Cybersecurity

Offensive

Offensive cybersecurity is the process of identifying, testing and bypassing security measures.

Offensive Roles:
  • Penetration Tester
  • Vulnerability Researcher
  • Ethical Hacker

Defensive

Defensive cybersecurity consists of prevention, detection, and mitigation of cyber attacks.

Defensive Roles:
  • Security Operations Center (SOC) Analyst
  • Incident Responder
  • Security Architect
